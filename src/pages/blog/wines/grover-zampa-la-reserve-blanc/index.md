###About
La Reserve  Blanc is a premium wine from Grover-Zampa which is based out of Nandi Hills, Karnataka & Nashik from Maharashtra. 

La réserve blanc is aged in french oak barrels, with the fragrant notes of honeysuckle, apricot and pineapple. rich and powerful with a long persistence, it finishes on tropical fruit, citrus and soft oak notes. It is created with the same passion as La Réserve Red, with dedication to quality and respect for traditions. It is produced from hand-picked and bunch pressed selection of Viognier grapes from our best parcel of vineyards, fermented in Barrels and then aged on lees.

La Réserve wines continue to charm the world with their class and elegance. As this majestic collection has made it to some of the most prestigious restaurants, numerous epicureans in over 20 different countries have enjoyed it.

###Wine Properties

####Color
Straw-Yellow
####Aromas
Fragrant notes of honeysuckle, apricot and pineapple.
####Palate
Rich and powerful with long finish, ending on tropical fruit, citrus and soft oak notes.
####Serving Temperature
Serve chilled at 8°C - 10°C
####Grape Variety
Viognier (Barrel Fermented)
