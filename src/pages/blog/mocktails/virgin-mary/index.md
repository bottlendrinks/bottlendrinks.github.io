###About
Virgin Mary is a non-alcoholic savory beverage with tomato juice, worcestershire sauce, tobasco, black pepper, salt and celery.The Virgin Mary keeps that same wonderful as Bloody Mary flavor without the vodka.

###Ingredients 
   - 4 Parts Tomato Juice
   - ⅔ Part Worcestershire Sauce
   - ⅔ Part Lemon Juice
   - 3 Dashes Hot Sauce
   - 1 Teaspoon Horseradish
   - 1 Pinch Celery Salt
   - 1 Pinch Ground black pepper
   - 1 Stick Celery
   - 1 Wedge Lemon

###How to prepare
   - Mix all liquids one after the other in high ball glass 
   - Add Ice & stirr well. 
   - Garnish with Celery 
   - optional: Add Pepper little amount 

###Available Places:
Available in Almost all the restaurants with Bar with more than three star rated in Zomato
