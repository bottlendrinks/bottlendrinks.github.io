###About
Virgin Mojito is the non alcoholic version of the famous Mojito. It’s destined for those that don’t drink alcohol but still desire to get the freshness given by the mint component of the drink. Fresh Mint leaves are antioxidant and anti-inflammatory in nature which helps you to get some relief from head aches. 

###Ingredients
   - 15 mint leaves
   - 1 teaspoon raw sugar
   - 1 ounce fresh lime juice 
   - Ice
   - 4 ounces of chilled ginger ale / Club Soda / Sparkling Water


###How to prepare
   - Take a Glass and Crush Mint leaves & lemon juice 
   - Add Simple Syrup  & Mix well. 
   - Top with GingerAle or Soda or Sparkling water. 
   - Add Ice & stirr well.
