###About
Virgin PinaColada is a non-alcoholic savory beverage which is most commonly available drink in beaches where temperatures are high. it is  smoothie drink which is made of pineapple juice, coconut milk, frozen pineapple, sugar and chilled water. This will bring instant energy during summer because of coconut milk which is present inside the smoothie.

###Ingredients 
   - 1 cup of pineapple Juice
   - 2 cups of frozen pineapple
   - 1 cup of coconut Milk
   - 1/3 cup of coconut cream(optional)

###How to prepare
   - Place the pineapple juice, frozen pineapple, and coconut cream in the blender. 
   - Turn on and blend until smooth. 
   - If you would like your pina colada sweeter, Add some honey or sugar

###Available Places:
Available in Almost all the restaurants with Bar with more than three star rated in Zomato
